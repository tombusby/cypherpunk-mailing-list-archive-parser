CREATE STUNNING EDGE EFFECTS FOR IMAGES WITH PHOTOFRAME

Digital Edge Effects. Each volume contains over 150 professionally
designed frames.
http://www.extensis.com/press/980217.html

http://www.extensis.com/press/980202.html

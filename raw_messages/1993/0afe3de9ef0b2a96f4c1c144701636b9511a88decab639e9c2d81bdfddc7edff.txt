McGraw Hill's *Data Communications* magazine (int'l edition) of
August '93 has a long and informative article on the Clipper
issue.

Reference:

  "Clinton's Clipper: Can It Keep a Secret?
   - The administration's new encryption chip should have been
     code-named 'Controversy'"
   by Salvatore Salamone
   *Data Communications*, August 1993
   pp 53-54, 56, 58

   Vol. 22, No. 11
   ISSN 0363-6399

No mention is made of PGP.  However, general DES and RSA are
covered in some detail, with some mention made of DSS.

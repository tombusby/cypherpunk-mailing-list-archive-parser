Greetings from ZDNet!

We're pleased to announce several exciting new features 
debuting on ZDNet this week! The new features include:

-- A dynamic new site design
-- Cool and useful Java-based viewers for product reviews
-- A new slate of software application classes at ZDU
-- 2 new groundbreaking sites: HealthyPC and LaunchPad.
 
Please read on for details and visit us today to check
out the latest additions to ZDNet, rated the #1 News, Information
and Entertainment Web site by PC Meter for seven months 
in a row!

We've redesigned the site to  speed navigation through our

to find quickly find the information you need using our
sophisticated "Find It" feature.

ZDNet:	                  http://www5.zdnet.com/

ZDNet Products:   http://www5.zdnet.com/products/

ZDNet University:   http://www.zdu/com
